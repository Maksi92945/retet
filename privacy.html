Privacy Policy

Effective: June 4, 2026 · Last updated: June 4, 2026

The short version

We collect your email, a nickname, and a hashed password to run your account. Your AI conversations are processed to generate a reply but not used to train models. We don't sell your data and don't run third-party ad trackers. You can delete your account at any time.

1. Who we are

Alma is a content research and AI assistant tool for creators, solopreneurs and marketers. The service is operated by [Your Name or Company], reachable at [support@your-domain.com]. For privacy questions specifically: [privacy@your-domain.com].

For the purposes of GDPR (EU) and similar laws, we are the data controller of the personal data we process about you.

2. What we collect

Account data

  • Email address — used to sign you in and send verification codes.
  • Nickname — displayed in your profile.
  • Password — stored as a bcrypt hash (cost factor 12). We never see or store your plaintext password.

Usage data

  • IP address — recorded temporarily for rate limiting and abuse prevention.
  • Last activity timestamp — to show "active 7 days" stats and identify dormant accounts.
  • Subscription status and daily token counter.
  • Audit log of privileged admin actions (block, delete, change subscription, etc.).

Content you create

  • Boards and items (notes, ideas, URLs) — stored server-side, tied to your account.
  • Drafts, plans, voice profiles, creator watchlists — stored in your browser's localStorage only. These never reach our servers.
  • AI messages — your input is forwarded to the AI engine to produce a reply. See section 4.

Verification codes

  • Six-digit codes emailed to you during sign-up or sign-in. They expire after 10 minutes and are deleted automatically.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not store your plaintext password.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not run advertising trackers or third-party analytics pixels.
  • We do not log the request bodies of API calls — your AI messages do not appear in server log files.

3. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To generate AI responses based on the messages you send.
  • To deliver email verification codes.
  • To enforce rate limits and detect abuse.
  • To meter and bill your token usage.
  • To send essential operational emails about your account (verification, billing, security).

4. AI processing

When you send a message to the AI assistant, the message text and any attached media are forwarded to our AI engine. Depending on configuration this may be a self-hosted model (Ollama on our infrastructure) or a third-party API such as [name your provider, e.g. Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic]. The third-party provider may temporarily process this content to generate the response according to their privacy policy.

We do not use your AI conversations or any content you upload to train any AI model. If a third-party provider's default policy includes training, we configure their API to opt out where that option exists.

5. Cookies and local storage

We use:

  • localStorage to remember your session token and UI preferences.
  • No third-party cookies.
  • No advertising or behavioural-tracking cookies.

Because we do not set tracking cookies, no cookie consent banner is shown. If we add analytics in the future, we will ask for your consent first.

6. Data retention

  • Account data: kept until you delete your account.
  • Audit log: kept indefinitely as an operational and security record.
  • Verification codes: auto-deleted 10 minutes after issuance.
  • Sessions: JWT tokens expire after 30 days; can be revoked instantly by the admin or via "Force logout".
  • Billing records: retained as long as required by tax / accounting law in our jurisdiction (typically 5–7 years).

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have some or all of the following rights:

  • Access — view the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — fix inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your account and associated data.
  • Export — receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, by deleting your account.
  • Object — to a specific use of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email [privacy@your-domain.com]. We respond within 30 days.

EU residents (GDPR): you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Russian residents (152-ФЗ): you have additional rights regarding the processing of your personal data. Personal data of Russian citizens may be stored on servers located in the Russian Federation as required by law.

8. Security

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12).
  • All authenticated endpoints require a valid signed JWT.
  • HTTP responses include strict security headers — Content-Security-Policy, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy.
  • Suspicious activity triggers automatic rate limiting; admin actions are logged.
  • Production traffic runs over HTTPS.

No system can be 100% secure. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it responsibly to [security@your-domain.com].

9. Children

Alma is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes we will notify registered users by email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Alma after the update means you accept the new policy.
